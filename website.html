About Me

I'm Nancy Bakhaty, a Mechatronics Engineer developing expertise in product development, design engineering, and electronics. By having this website, I can build and document engineering projects alongside my work experience to keep my technical skills sharp, demonstrate practical problem-solving, take ownership, and track my own growth.

My work sits at the intersection of mechanical design, software, and embedded hardware. I design in 3D, program in Python, simulate in ROS 2 while integrating hardware, all while approaching each problem as a system, not a single discipline, making my work go from concept to working implementation systems/products. Not only am I working on my engineering projects, critical thinking, and showcasing my commitment to growth, but I also built and designed this website from scratch as a live record of some of my technical abilities.
