Robotics with ROS 2

Exploring practical robotic applications with the Robot Operating System (ROS), including navigation, simulation, and hardware. This section documents my applied learning for ROS 2 simulations and hardware integrations, starting from the beginner level all the way to advanced, one project at a time and covering robot control, parameter management, and embedded hardware deployment. Part of my process is to draw from multiple references and sources, adapting and extending them to my own logic and design decisions. All work is to demonstrate my capabilities, which is shared for showcase purposes only and may not be reproduced or used commercially.
